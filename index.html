Multimodailty

Using different modes to represent information. Images, videos, text, all used to create a multimodal document to inform and educate.

Affordances

How people react to digital rhetoric, what it offers in terms of products and services. Affordances are what the digital rhetoric becomes, and it's limits are discovered.

Digital Rhetoric

The foundation to multimodality, as well as discovering its affordances. Digital Rhetoric plugs this all together through using digital media and persuading or informing audiences a certain way.

Modalities

Digital Rhetoric does more than entertain, but it allows those interacting with it to feel. These are just a few of those modalities.

Hear

Multimodality allows for users to hear and listen to information being presented, and not necessarily read it like we are used to. There are other ways to immerse a user by using sound and voices.

See

The most obvious way, but for accessibility it makes an interesting turn to how users can still see without actually using their eyes.

Feel

Digital Rhetoric can be very emotional in its persuasion, making users feel a certain way. The power of using digital rhetoric can alter a users emotions to follow exactly what the program whats them to feel.
